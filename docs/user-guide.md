## Time Zone Converter üåç

### Overview
The Time Zone Converter helps you coordinate across global time zones. Perfect for international teams, remote workers, and global businesses.

**Features:**
- Convert time between 195+ countries and territories
- Compare multiple time zones simultaneously
- Interactive calendar with time picker
- Reverse timezone calculator (Romania-focused)
- UTC offset display
- Support for major cities within countries (Sydney, Melbourne, Toronto, etc.)

### Accessing Time Zone Converter
1. Click "Time Zone Converter" in the main menu
2. Or navigate to `/time-zone` page
3. The converter opens in full-page view

### How to Use Time Zone Converter

#### Step 1: Select Date and Time
1. On the left side, you'll see an interactive calendar
2. Click on any date to select it
3. Use the time picker to set the hour and minute (24-hour format)
4. The selected date and time display below the calendar as: `YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM`

**Example:** Selecting December 15, 2024 at 14:30 will show "2024-12-15 14:30"

#### Step 2: Add Countries/Timezones
1. Look for the "Add Time Zone" dropdown on the right side
2. Click the dropdown to open the search
3. Type to search for a country (e.g., "Japan", "Germany", "Australia")
4. Click on a country from the search results to add it
5. The timezone for that country is automatically added to your comparison

**Tips:**
- Search is case-insensitive
- You can search by country name or timezone name
- Already selected timezones won't appear in the list again

#### Step 3: View Time Conversions
Below the calendar, you'll see timezone cards displaying:
- **Country/Region Name** - Shows the selected location
- **Time in 24-Hour Format** - The converted time (e.g., 20:45)
- **Full Date and Day** - Complete date information (e.g., Monday, December 15)
- **UTC Offset** - Difference from UTC (e.g., UTC +01:00)

**Example Card Display:**

#### Step 4: Remove Timezone
- To remove a timezone, click the "Remove" button on its card
- The card will slide up and disappear
- Your calendar view will update to show fewer timezones

### Tips for Effective Time Zone Coordination
1. **Double-Check Time Conversions**: Always verify critical meeting times
2. **Use Descriptive Labels**: Label timezone cards with meeting details
3. **Plan Ahead**: Schedule meetings considering all participants' time zones
4. **Regular Updates**: Keep your timezone settings updated
